Terms and condition

1.Candidates should make the application with the consent and approval of their parents/guardians.

2.They must affirm that the information given in the application is complete and true to the best of their complete and true to the best of their knowledge..

3.They must undertake to abide by the disciplinary rules and regulations of the University.

4.The candidates are also expected to undertake to abide by the regulation regarding course curriculum and academic standards as may be prescribed by the University from time to time.

5.The candidates shall not do anything either inside or outside the University premises that will interfere with the day-to-day function of university management, decorum, and discipline. Candidates must understand that if the information provided above is not true, their admission to the course may be canceled at any stage.
